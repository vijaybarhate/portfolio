# Design System

## Core Theme

### Colors
- **Background:** `hsl(0, 0%, 4%)`
- **Surface:** `hsl(0, 0%, 8%)`
- **Text Primary:** `hsl(0, 0%, 96%)`
- **Text Muted:** `hsl(0, 0%, 53%)`
- **Stroke/Border:** `hsl(0, 0%, 12%)`
- **Accent:** `hsl(0, 0%, 96%)`
- **Accent Gradient:** `linear-gradient(90deg, #89AACC 0%, #4E85BF 100%)`

### Typography
- **Body Font:** Inter (300-700)
- **Display Font:** Instrument Serif (italic, 400)

### Motion
- Smooth GSAP scroll transitions
- Framer Motion page reveals
- Custom keyframe animations for gradients and loaders.
