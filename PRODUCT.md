# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Recruiters, hiring managers, and fellow developers looking for a Python Developer & Data Analyst.

## Product Purpose
To showcase Vijay Dilip Barhate's practical, data-driven applications, clean code, skills, and certifications, ultimately securing an internship or entry-level role.

## Brand Personality
Bold, dark, polished, technically sharp, and highly animated. It should feel modern, alive, and professional, avoiding generic AI aesthetics.

## Anti-references
Generic cream/sand/beige SaaS themes. Identical card grids. Typical light-mode minimalist portfolios. Sketchy SVG illustrations.

## Design Principles
- Show, don't tell: Prove skills through highly polished UI and interactions.
- Bold aesthetics: Embrace a dark, tech-focused design with an accent gradient.
- Meaningful motion: Every animation must feel intentional and smooth.
- Impeccable contrast: Ensure all text is readable against the dark background.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Ensure semantic HTML, high contrast text (>=4.5:1), and consider reduced motion preferences.
